What are we doing?


One of the most interesting, challenging and meaningful subjects of these days is cities sustainability. This term may embrace many concepts. How harmful is the city towards the planet? Can the city survive on its own or has the resources to share the wealth? Can the city be environmentally, socially and economically friendly?
Of course, covering the whole available metrics is tough, so in this project we'll uncover some insights about social aspects of sustainable cities. The big 100 are the most influetial and populated cities across the globe. You can see the whole data set under data tab.
